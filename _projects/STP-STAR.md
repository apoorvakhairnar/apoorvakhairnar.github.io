**The Goal:** Design a cost-effective, lightweight Static Test Pad (STP) for characterizing thrust curves and specific impulse of various rocket motors.

**My Role/The Solution:** As Systems Engineer, I coordinated teams and developed conceptual designs and animations in Fusion 360 for a unique STP accommodating various motor sizes.

**Tools:** Fusion 360, Systems Engineering

**Link:** [ResearchGate Report](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/357554998_Internship_Report_on_STATIC_TEST_PAD_FOR_ROCKET_MOTOR_At_STAR_-_Space_Technology_and_Aeronautical_Rocketry)
