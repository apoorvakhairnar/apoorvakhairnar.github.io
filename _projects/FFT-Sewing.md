**The Goal:** Understand how the vibration signals of a sewing machine vary under various conditions using Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) Analysis.

**My Role/The Solution:** Conducted FFT analysis of vibration signals under regular maintenance, poor maintenance, and induced fault conditions (loosened bobbin, unbalanced hand wheel, loosened feed dog).

**Tools:** Fast Fourier Transform (FFT), Vibration Analysis

**Link:** [ResearchGate Report](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/357554574_FFT_Analysis_of_Traditional_Sewing_Machine_Signal)
