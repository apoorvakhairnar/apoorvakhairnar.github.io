**The Goal:** Study and understand the applications of numerical methods utilized in the design and optimization of surface textures for tilting pad thrust bearings.

**My Role/The Solution:** Analyzed the use of the Newton-Raphson method for optimizing surface texturing parameters of tilting pad thrust bearings, focusing on practical application.

**Tools:** Numerical Methods, Newton-Raphson Method
