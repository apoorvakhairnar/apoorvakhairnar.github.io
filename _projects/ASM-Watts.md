**The Goal:** Analyze and synthesize a Four-bar mechanism called the Watt's Linkage.

**My Role/The Solution:** Analyzed the mechanism using Freudenstein's method and graphical synthesis, verified results with velocity/acceleration diagrams, and fabricated a physical prototype using cardboard and styrofoam.

**Tools:** Freudenstein's Method, Graphical Synthesis, Physical Prototyping

**Link:** [ResearchGate Report](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/382424672_Analysis_and_Synthesis_of_Four-bar_Mechanism_-_Watt's_Linkage)
