**The Goal:** Develop a Non-Pyrotechnic recovery system for high-powered model rockets to improve reliability and reduce cost compared to pyrotechnic systems.

**My Role/The Solution:** As a Systems Engineer, I coordinated the Design and Avionics teams, developed the conceptual design, and performed modelling and animation in Fusion 360.

**Tools:** Fusion 360, Systems Engineering
