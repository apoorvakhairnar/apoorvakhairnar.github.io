**The Goal:** Design and fabricate an innovative soft robotic gripper capable of picking up various objects.

**My Role/The Solution:** Co-developed a unique gripper combining Kirigami flexibility and Vacuum Jamming stiffness using coffee beans. Successfully gripped objects ranging from a paper crane to a soda can.

**Tools:** Soft Robotics, Kirigami, Vacuum Jamming
